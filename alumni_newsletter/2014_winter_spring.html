Volunteer at SLC in March

The Indiana FBLA-PBL State Leadership Conference will be held on March 14-16, 2014 at the Sheraton Hotel, Keystone Crossing in Indianapolis. The state committee strives to secure business professionals that can help our members become prepared for their career choices and the national competition.

We ask for your assistance as a professional and impartial judge for our Indiana high school students from around all areas of Indiana.

The bulk of performance events will take place on Saturday, March 15th however, there are also events that require volunteers on Friday night March 14th.

Please visit the link below to review a list of competitive events and sign up.

Sponsor an SLC event!

Not able to make it to SLC this year? Consider sponsoring a competitive event. With sponsorship levels starting at just $20 for competitive events, you can support Indiana FBLA-PBL and gain awareness for your business.

State Recognition Update

For the third year, Indiana FBLA is officially recognized by the Indiana Dept. of Education. Indiana FBLA has a seat on the coordinating council which is made-up of the seven career technical student organization directors: Skills USA, FFA, HOSA, DECA, BPA, and FCCLA

The infrastructure grant provided has been a tremendous help to FBLA-PBL in Indiana as we have been able to purchase equipment for conference, provide adviser training days, travel for recruitment etc.
